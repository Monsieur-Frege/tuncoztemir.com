At iQpay

I run the product, engineering, and data behind a payments platform operating at national scale.

iQpay decides, in real time and product by product, what health and social programs cover at the register, across hundreds of programs and tens of thousands of retailers. I own the intelligence behind those decisions: the classification pipelines that make them, the evaluation systems that keep them honest, and the controls that let them run in production against a live payment network, at a standard of correctness that leaves no room for error.

What I build
Precision

Turning no into yes.

A payment network that could only refuse products can, in my systems, be trusted to allow them. I built the precision that unlocked entire categories the network had never been able to offer, and a fresh-produce eligibility standard adopted by a state health authority and now live across a national program.

Trust

Judgment you can leave alone.

Decisions that move money cannot be supervised by hand at scale. I build multi-model juries, calibrated evaluations, and release gates that make automated judgment reliable enough to run unattended, and honest enough to raise its hand the moment it should not.

Foundation

The data beneath it all.

Seven million products, unified from national retail feeds, live sources, and field data I gathered in person, into the single platform every eligibility decision and every invoice draws on.
